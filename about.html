About Randi

An aspiring statistician and G.I.S. enthusiast, Randi Bolt is an undergrad student at Portland State University who has been passionately dedicated to developing her crossover skills in mathematics and mapping to uncover popular and potentially unusual trends in data sets. When she is not participating in ASPRS events, seminars, PDXWIT and G.I.S. club she actively advocates for intersectional feminism, destigmatizing cannabis, and BLM.
